THE BASICS

What makes a car a hybrid

A hybrid uses two power sources together, and cleverly reuses energy that a normal car wastes.

01 · ENGINE

Combustion engine

A petrol engine provides strong power for high speeds and long distances — the dominant source on the straights.

02 · ELECTRIC

Electric motor

An electric motor adds instant torque and can drive the car on its own at low speed, cutting fuel use and emissions.

03 · RECOVERY

Regenerative braking

When braking, the motor works as a generator and stores the energy in the battery — energy a normal car would lose as heat.
